Renderer

How to Play

  • Move 😃 with WASD/HJKL/⬅⬆⬇➡ Keys
  • Collect ⭐'s to win points
  • Run away from 💀's to avoid death

About the Game

I started programming at age twelve when an older student at my school showed me that all you really needed to build video games was a laptop and an idea. I spent months tinkering with Game Maker , building as many games as I could to show my friends. SKULL is a remake of the first of those games, built with HTML5 canvas and Javascript, it follows in the footsteps of its predecessor by using no art what so ever, relying on emojis rather than custom sprites.
