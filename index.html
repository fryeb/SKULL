Controls

  • Collect ⭐'s to win points

About

A re-creation of my very first game. While I used Game Maker and its stock sprites to make the original,this re-make uses JavaScript and HTML5, and re-purposes your browsers built-in emojis as sprites to breathe life into the game world.
